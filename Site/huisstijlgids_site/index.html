Introduction

These brand guidelines outline the brand values and brand elements which make up the Frontify brand. They include rules and values to help you compose and produce exciting designs with a high degree of creative flexibility.

Our Mission

Frontify's mission is to transform the documentation and the usage of brand guidelines into the digital age. Designers, frontend developers and brand managers use Frontify to become more efficient and improve brand consistency over all channels.

Brand Values

At Frontify, we're passionate about making things simple and joyful. We try hard to do so in every work we do. But we also know, that in this case, perfectness is like 42 :) Another important aspect is our Swiss background. The quality and reliability of our products is very important to us and - as we are convinced - even more important for you. Facing our customers, we give our best to be friendly and fair. That's it.
